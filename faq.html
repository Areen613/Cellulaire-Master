Repairs & Pricing

Everything about costs and repair times

Q

How long does a typical repair take?

Most screen and battery replacements are done the same day, often within a couple of hours. Water damage or board-level work can take longer because we need more testing time. We'll give you an estimated timeframe when you drop off your device.

Q

Do you use original or aftermarket parts?

We work with high-quality OEM-grade parts from trusted suppliers and test every part in store. If there is more than one option for your device, we'll explain the difference in price and quality so you can make an informed decision.

Q

Can you give me a quote before I come?

Yes! Give us the exact model (for example iPhone 14 Pro Max or Galaxy S23) and what happened. We can usually give a price range by phone and confirm it once we see the device.

Q

Is there a warranty on the repair?

Repairs come with a limited warranty against defects in parts or workmanship. Physical damage, liquid damage or future drops are not covered. Ask us about specific warranty terms when you drop off your device.
